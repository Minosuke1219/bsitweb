Cash Donations

For cash donations, you can follow these steps

  1. Go to our cash donations form for you to fill up
  2. You will need to authorize your Gcash account
    in order to continue
  3. You will then proceed to our payment service provider Gcash
  4. Once the form is complete, hit submit
  5. You will also download the copy of your form as
    a proof for your donation

Goods and Supplies

For Goods and Supplies donations,
you can follow these steps

  1. The picture above is where the Barangay Minuyan III located
  2. The barangay is only open at 8:00Am to 10:00PM
  3. You may browse our News and Current Events section for
    you to see if there are donation drives that are open
    (if you want to donate specifically)
  4. The goods and supplies must be delivered to the barangay
  5. The staff will take care of your donations
FAQ's
  1. What if there are no donation drives that are open?
    • You can still go to our barangay and bring your goods and supplies, the donations given will be placed in our donations room for safe keeping. Once a donation drive is open, the goods and supplies will be distributed to that donation drive.
  2. What if I'm at the barangay can I still donate for cash?
    • Yes you can, our barangay has an internet access so you can open your Gcash account and donate some money
  3. I don't know where barangay Minuyan III is located
    • The link below will redirect you to Google maps to guide you in your destination
